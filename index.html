About

Spin

SPIN - Space Innovation is a nonprofit organisation with a strong focus on the development and knowledge-sharing of space technologies, consisting mainly of university students. We believe that scientific knowledge about space should be readily accessible to young people!

SPIN's vision is to enable students and researchers to engage in space technologies involving them in the process of planning and implementing of innovative programs in order to reinforce the Greek Space Ecosystem. Our goal is to strengthen the culture of space exploration in the country, creating a space technologies development core.

What we do

Projects

  • CanSat in Greece

    CanSat in Greece is a greek space competition held in collaboration with the European Space Agency (ESA). Participants are tasked to design and build an educational can-sized satellite for the purpose of carrying out a space mission that they themselves have envisioned.

    Learn more

  • Project Rocketry

    SPIN’s Rocketry Team is engaged in research and development of smallsat launcher systems. SPIN was the first organization to successfully launch fully student researched and developed high power rockets in Greece carrying CanSats to an altitude of 1km.

    Learn more

  • STEM Workshops

    SPIN has many years of experience in organizing STEM Workshops designed for high school & university students as well as high school teachers incorporating Arduino technologies. So far, 40 STEM seminars have been organized across Greece with more than 600 participants.

    Learn more

  • Space Talks

    Throughout the year SPIN organizes Space Talks that focus on knowledge sharing between participants, academics & industry leaders in the space sector. During these events participants can get informed, meet like minded individuals and get inspired to follow a career in the space sector.

    Learn more
